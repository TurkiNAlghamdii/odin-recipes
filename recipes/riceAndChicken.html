Rice And Chicken

A meal we call in saudi a Kabsa

It's a main meal of rice and chicken usually eaten with a salad and hot sauce

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • chicken
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onions
  • Tomato
  • salt

Steps

  1. Clean the rice
  2. Start cocking the rice in boiled water
  3. Cut up the unions and Tomatos
  4. Put what you've cut with the rice with adding tomato sauce
  5. add salt
  6. Add the chicken with the rice
